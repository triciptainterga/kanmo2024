Dear Valued Partners,

 

Greetings from Nespresso Indonesia!


Due to the recent global situation, specifically the fluctuating exchange rate between IDR and the Swiss Franc along with rising shipping costs, we find it necessary to make adjustments to our Retail Prices. This decision ensures our continued commitment to providing value while navigating these challenging circumstances.


Effective March 1st, 2024, our new pricing structure has been implemented in the market. However, as a gesture of appreciation for our enduring partnership, we offer you the opportunity to purchase at the current prices until April 1st, 2024, or until the expiration of any existing signed contracts.

Please be assured that we have kept any increases to an absolute minimum.


For further information and assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to your dedicated Sales Representative.
